Born in Athens in 1978.
In 1986, he studied electronic church organ and music theory in the conservatory of Kallithea (Athens). He graduated in 1996, obtaining the corresponding degrees. From 1997, he is an autodidact at the music intruments: guitar, electric bass and percussion, while studying Economics and Management at the Piraeus Institute. From 2000, his contact with the creation of music through Technology & Computers and acquires full familiarization with music software (Cakewalk Sonar, Cubase, Soundforge etc). In 2005, after his military service, he attended higher studies in music theory and music analysis courses with Professor Dionisis Alamanos. In 2007, he completed his studies in contrapuntal composition and proceeded to fugue and orchestration in the "Orfeio Conservatory of Athens." He is experienced in the music composition and soundtrack of film, theater and other audiovisual and performing projects:

*"Timor Mortis" Short Film / Drama International Festival (2008).
*"The little others" feature documentary / Thessaloniki International Doc. Festival (2011)/ Chalkida Doc.Festival (2011).
*"Mirror" stage play/ dir. Nikos Drosakis (2011).
*"The Frames"- stage monologue / Athens Fringe Festival (2012).
*"Creation of a collaborative network of representatives of immigrant associations at European Level"-Audiovisual part of EU social project (as a music composer).

He has numerous performances in music stages in Athens, participating in music groups as a keyboards, guitar and bass guitar player. Since 2010, he is a permanent music partner of "TABULA RASA ARTES MUNDI" artistic and social consortium.
